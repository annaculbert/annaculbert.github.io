About Me

Hi, my name is Anna Culbert and I am a healthcare professional turned data enthusiast located in the foothills of Boulder, Colorado. I have recently earned my professional certificate in data analytics through Google’s Career Certificate Platform and am eager to fully immerse myself into the world of data.

I attended the University of Missouri where I received my Bachelor of Health Science degree as well as my master’s degree in occupational therapy in 2017. As I progressed through my career, learning the ins and outs of the profession and developing more awareness of my strengths, passions, and career goals, I found myself searching for what other fields could offer. As I explored what would better fulfill my aspirations, I quickly realized that data analytics was something I felt excited and eager to learn more about.

I love that working with data gives you structure and facts to analyze, but a story to tell to make sense of it all. It can help guide businesses and organizations of any sort through the many decisions to be made and paths to be taken, all with data-driven recommendations. The challenges, the puzzles, the patterns, and the opportunity for continuous self-learning is what excites me for this new chapter in my life that I’m dedicated to succeeding at. This page is just my beginning and I can’t wait for the projects to grow.
